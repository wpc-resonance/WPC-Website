About

Wild Petal Collective

Wild Petal Collective is rooted in the Sierra landscape — where wild growth, mountain light, and natural textures shape the way we see beauty.

We create botanical environments and personal talismans designed to bring elegance, presence, and quiet meaning into everyday life. Through plant design, natural materials, and symbolic pieces shaped by time, Wild Petal exists between space and self — what surrounds you, and what stays with you.

Wild Petal Talismans are patent pending objects shaped by time, touch, and quiet reflection.
