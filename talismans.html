What It Is

Not a tracker. Not a dashboard. Not another screen.

A quiet object you touch to return to yourself.

Wild Petal Talismans are wearable pieces designed around gentle signals, tactile rings, and symbolic growth over time.

The Interaction

Touch → Reflect → Stay

  1. TouchTouch your talisman.
  2. ReflectReceive a subtle haptic response and notice whether you feel open or closed.
  3. StayStay with yourself for a moment; over time, rings form.

Rings Over Time

Like rings on a tree, each layer is earned slowly.

The rings are not a score. They are not a mood chart. They are not a performance metric.

They are a tactile record of time spent returning to yourself.
